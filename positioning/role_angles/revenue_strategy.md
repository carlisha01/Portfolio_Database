# Role Angle: Revenue Strategy

Use this file when applying to roles such as: Revenue Analyst, Revenue Strategy Analyst, Yield Management Analyst, Pricing Analyst, Junior Finance Business Partner (revenue-adjacent), commercial strategy roles.

---

## Core Pitch (3 sentences)

I developed my first interest in revenue management during my internship at Majestic Hotel Group in Barcelona, where I worked with daily cross-property data on reservations, revenue, and F&B income. I have since applied revenue and commercial thinking to restaurant operations — working on product profitability analysis, sales trend dashboards, and cost-margin matching between supplier purchase data and restaurant sales. I am looking to build on this foundation in a revenue analyst or commercial strategy role.

---

## What to Foreground

- Majestic Hotel Group Revenue Management Internship — daily cross-property data, reservations, revenue, F&B income, Excel reporting, dashboard support
- Restaurant Operations Dashboard — sales trends, daypart revenue, peak hours, revenue visibility
- Product Matching and Cost Analysis — margin analysis, supplier cost vs. sales price, profitability framework
- KPI frameworks — defining and tracking revenue and performance indicators
- Data structuring and Excel skills — revenue-relevant data handling
- SQL — querying and aggregating revenue and sales data from relational tables (DataCamp Associate Data Analyst in SQL track, in progress)
- Rocxi Beach cost & profitability web app — built a tool (PostgreSQL/SQL) that crosses POS sales with recipe costs to show real margin per product and family
- Commercial awareness from hospitality — understanding how pricing, demand, and product mix affect revenue

## What to Downplay

- CRM and customer segmentation (mention briefly)
- Automation details (mention as a skill, not a main story)
- Early hospitality operations (briefly as context)

---

## Language to Use

- "Worked with reservations, revenue, and F&B income data at Majestic Hotel Group"
- "Structured cross-property performance data for revenue management reporting"
- "Analysed sales trends to identify peak revenue periods and underperforming dayparts"
- "Developed a product profitability framework connecting supplier costs with sales data"
- "Defined KPIs for revenue visibility and management reporting"
- "Identified margin opportunities through product and category-level cost analysis"
- "Commercial awareness grounded in real hospitality and restaurant operations"

## Language to Avoid

- Starting with hospitality operations as the main pitch
- Vague claims about Excel or data without specifics
- Downplaying the Majestic revenue management role (it is the anchor for this angle)
