# Cover Letter Closing Statements — Carla Manzano Ramells

Select and adapt based on tone and context. Always confident, never apologetic.

---

## Standard Professional Closing

> I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my background in operations, data analysis, and digital transformation could contribute to [Company]. I am available for a conversation at your convenience and can provide portfolio materials or references on request.

---

## For Roles Where Cultural Fit Matters

> Beyond the technical fit, I am drawn to [Company] because [specific reason — mission, approach, industry, team]. I would be glad to discuss this further and show you the work I have been building.

---

## For Competitive / Selective Programmes

> I understand that [Programme / Role] is competitive, and I am not applying because I need a role. I am applying because this is exactly the context where I can develop fastest and contribute most. I would value the chance to make that case directly.

---

## For Hospitality / Operations Roles

> Hospitality is a field where the best people do not just know the service standards — they understand the operational logic behind them and how to improve it. That is what I bring, and I would be glad to demonstrate it in a conversation.

---

## For Digital Transformation / Tech-Adjacent Roles

> I am at a stage where I can grow quickly in the right environment — not because I have every technical skill already, but because I have the operational context to know what those skills are actually for. I would welcome the chance to explore that fit with [Company].

---

## Short and Direct Closing (for companies with a concise, no-filler culture)

> I would like to discuss this role. I am available at [email / phone] and happy to share work samples or references.

---

## Closing With Specific Availability

> I am currently based in Barcelona and available to start [date/timeframe]. I would welcome a call or meeting to discuss the role in more detail.
