## How to Add a New Application

1. Copy the last row and increment the `#`
2. Fill in: Company, Role, Role Type (use one of the 6 role families from `positioning/role_angles/`), Date Applied
3. Update Stage as it progresses
4. Fill Outcome when resolved: Offer / Rejected / Withdrawn / No Response
5. Use Notes for anything useful: recruiter name, specific feedback, follow-up needed, what angle was used
