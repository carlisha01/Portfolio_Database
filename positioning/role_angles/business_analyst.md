# Role Angle: Business Analyst

Use this file when applying to roles such as: Business Analyst, Junior Business Analyst, Business Operations Analyst, Data Quality Analyst, Junior BI Analyst, Process Analyst.

---

## Core Pitch (3 sentences)

I have built analytical skills in real operational contexts, not only in academic settings. My work at Rocxi Beach and my revenue management internship at Majestic Hotel Group gave me direct experience structuring data, designing KPI frameworks, building dashboards, and turning messy information into decisions. I am looking to apply these skills in a business analyst role where I can support teams with structured reporting, process documentation, and data-driven insight.

---

## What to Foreground

- Revenue management internship at Majestic Hotel Group — daily data, cross-property reporting, Excel structuring
- Restaurant Operations Dashboard project — KPI definition, Looker Studio / Power BI, sales trend analysis
- Customer Feedback Analysis System — categorisation framework, AI-assisted structuring, pattern detection
- Product Matching and Cost Analysis — cross-file data matching, margin analysis, structured output
- Digital Transformation Roadmap (EAE) — process mapping, gap analysis, requirements-style thinking
- Excel advanced skills, data cleaning, data structuring
- SQL — querying, joining and aggregating relational data (DataCamp Associate Data Analyst in SQL track, in progress)
- Rocxi Beach cost & profitability web app — modelled a relational PostgreSQL database and built reporting on real cost and sales data (built with Claude Code)
- AI tools used for structured analysis, not as a crutch

## What to Downplay

- Hospitality operational details (mention briefly as context, not as the main story)
- New Year's Eve experience (only if the role has a customer experience angle)
- Riyadh (mention briefly as international mobility, not as a defining experience)

---

## Language to Use

- "Structured data from X source into a dashboard / report / framework"
- "Defined KPIs for..."
- "Identified patterns in customer feedback / sales data / operational data"
- "Cleaned and matched data from [source A] and [source B]"
- "Queried, joined and aggregated data using SQL to answer [business question]"
- "Mapped current-state process and identified improvement opportunities"
- "Used AI tools to support data analysis, with validation applied at each step"
- "Built a reporting structure that..."
- "Translated operational complexity into..."

## Language to Avoid

- "I worked in hospitality" as the main opening
- "I am passionate about data" without grounding it in a specific project
- Vague claims about Excel or AI without context

---

## ATS Keywords to Include

business analysis, KPI reporting, data analysis, SQL, SQL queries, joins, data aggregation, process mapping, operational reporting, dashboard, Looker Studio, Power BI, Excel, data cleaning, data structuring, business intelligence, insight generation, stakeholder communication, process improvement
