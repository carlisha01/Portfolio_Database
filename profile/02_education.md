## Digital Transformation for SMEs Programme
**EAE Business School — Barcelona**
**Next Generation EU programme — 2025/2026 period**

Carla completed a Digital Transformation for SMEs programme at EAE Business School, developed within the Next Generation EU framework. The programme focused on how small and medium-sized companies can identify digital gaps, improve internal processes, use data more effectively, and implement digital tools in a realistic and business-oriented way.

For this programme, Carla developed a **complete digital transformation plan for a real SME**, using Rocxi Beach (her family restaurant) as the practical business case. This allowed her to connect digital transformation theory with a real operational environment where inefficiencies, customer data gaps, reservation workflows, feedback loops, and reporting gaps could be directly observed and addressed.

**Main areas covered:**
- Digital maturity analysis
- SME process mapping
- Business process improvement
- CRM strategy
- Automation opportunities
- KPI definition and dashboard planning
- AI and data use cases
- Change management considerations
- Practical digital transformation roadmap design

This programme reinforced Carla's interest in becoming a bridge between operations and technology. She became increasingly focused on how businesses can adopt digital tools in ways that are practical, understandable, and useful for the people actually running the operation.
