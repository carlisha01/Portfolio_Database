# Role Angle: CRM Operations

Use this file when applying to roles such as: CRM Operations Specialist, CRM Analyst, Sales Operations Admin, Customer Data Analyst, Customer Success Associate, Client Success Associate.

---

## Core Pitch (3 sentences)

I have worked directly with CoverManager — a hospitality CRM — handling reservation data, customer profiles, feedback exports, and post-visit survey workflows. I have developed a segmentation strategy to turn a large, underused customer database into an active business tool, and I have built an automation to manage recurring CRM report exports. I am looking to bring this hands-on CRM experience into a role focused on customer data, sales operations, or client success.

---

## What to Foreground

- CoverManager CRM — reservations, customer profiles, segmentation, feedback exports, best-client reports
- CRM Segmentation Strategy project — turning passive database into active tool
- Gmail-to-Drive Automation — automating CoverManager report management
- Customer Feedback Analysis — turning qualitative CRM data into operational insight
- Revenue Management internship — data structuring and customer-related reporting
- SQL — querying and segmenting customer data from relational tables (DataCamp Associate Data Analyst in SQL track, in progress)
- Customer sensibility from hospitality — understanding the person behind the data point

## What to Downplay

- Pure operations / F&B angle (mention briefly as context)
- Dashboard and BI tools (mention, but not as the main value)
- EAE programme (mention briefly)

---

## Language to Use

- "Worked with CoverManager CRM to analyse reservation data and customer profiles"
- "Designed a customer segmentation framework to enable personalised communication"
- "Automated recurring CRM report exports from Gmail to Google Drive"
- "Used customer feedback data to identify patterns and improve service decision-making"
- "Developed a strategy to move from a passive contact list to an active customer relationship tool"
- "Identified best-client segments based on frequency, history, and satisfaction data"

## Language to Avoid

- "I used CoverManager" without explaining what for
- Generic CRM claims not grounded in specific work done

## ATS Keywords to Include

CRM, CRM operations, customer relationship management, CoverManager, customer segmentation, customer data, customer profiling, SQL, data analysis, data-driven communication, personalisation, post-visit surveys, feedback management, customer insights, retention, loyalty, sales operations, customer success
