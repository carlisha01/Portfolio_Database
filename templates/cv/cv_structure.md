# CV Structure Guide — Carla Manzano Ramells

This guide defines the standard section order, length, and formatting rules for Carla's CVs. Adapt based on the role type using the relevant angle file in `positioning/role_angles/`.

---

## Standard CV Structure (European Format, 1 page)

### Section 1: Header
```
Carla Manzano Ramells
Barcelona, Spain | [email] | [phone] | [LinkedIn]
```
- Name: large, prominent
- Location: Barcelona, Spain
- No photo unless explicitly requested

### Section 2: Professional Headline
One line. Example:
> Junior Business Analyst | Hospitality Operations, CRM, Dashboards & AI-Assisted Process Improvement

Select from `positioning/cv_headlines.md` based on role type.

### Section 3: Professional Summary
3–4 lines. No filler. Connect operational background to the specific role.
- What she does
- Where her experience comes from
- What she is looking for

Select or adapt from `positioning/cv_headlines.md` (longer versions).

### Section 4: Key Skills
Group into 3–4 clusters. Examples:
- **Data & Analysis:** Excel (advanced), Google Sheets, data cleaning, KPI frameworks, data structuring
- **Dashboards & BI:** Looker Studio, Power BI, dashboard design, operational reporting
- **CRM & Automation:** CoverManager, Google Apps Script, Gmail/Drive automation, Zapier
- **AI & Tools:** ChatGPT, Claude, AI-assisted analysis, prompt structuring

Select skills relevant to the role. Do not list everything. 6–8 total entries per cluster.

### Section 5: Professional Experience
Reverse chronological. Each role:
```
Job Title | Company | Location | Dates
- Bullet 1 (action + outcome)
- Bullet 2 (action + outcome)
- Bullet 3 (action + outcome)
```

**Bullet point rules:**
- Start with an action verb (Built, Developed, Analysed, Designed, Automated, Structured, Identified)
- Include the outcome or business impact where possible
- Maximum 3–4 bullets per role
- Avoid passive voice and vague claims
- Select bullets from `templates/cv/achievement_bank.md`

**Experience order:** Use the angle file to determine which role leads.

### Section 6: Key Projects (optional — include when the role values portfolio work)
List 2–3 most relevant projects. Format:
```
Project Name | Tool(s) used
One-line description of problem solved and output.
```

### Section 7: Education
```
BA in Tourism and Hotel Management
IQS School of Management, Ramon Llull University — Barcelona | 2021–2026

Digital Transformation for SMEs (Next Generation EU)
EAE Business School — Barcelona | 2025–2026
```

### Section 8: Languages
```
Spanish (native) | Catalan (native) | English (C1 professional) | French (B1–B2)
```

---

## Length Guidelines

| Situation | Target length |
|---|---|
| Most junior roles | 1 page |
| Roles asking for detailed experience | 1–2 pages max |
| With projects section | 1.5 pages acceptable |

---

## ATS Optimisation Rules

1. Use keywords from `profile/07_keywords.md` that match the job description
2. Include the exact job title from the posting in the professional headline or summary
3. Do not use graphics, tables, or columns — ATS systems cannot always read them
4. Save/export as PDF unless the employer specifies Word format
5. File name format: `CV_Carla_Manzano_[CompanyName]_[RoleName].pdf`
