# Role Angle: Operations Analyst

Use this file when applying to roles such as: Operations Analyst, Business Operations Analyst, Process Improvement Analyst, Operations Coordinator, Junior Strategy and Transformation Coordinator, Sales Operations Analyst.

---

## Core Pitch (3 sentences)

I understand operations from the inside because I have worked in real service environments, not just studied them. At Rocxi Beach, I translated day-to-day operational experience into structured dashboards, process mapping, CRM improvement ideas, and automation workflows. I am looking to bring this combination of operational grounding and analytical skills to a business or operations analyst role.

---

## What to Foreground

- Real operational experience (Rocxi Beach, Majestic, early hospitality foundation) as credibility context
- Restaurant Operations Dashboard — process visibility, KPIs, operational decision-making
- Gmail-to-Drive Automation — identifying a real operational pain point and solving it with automation
- Digital Transformation Roadmap (EAE) — process mapping, current-state analysis, improvement planning
- Product Matching and Cost Analysis — operational data work connecting two data sources
- CRM workflow work — understanding how operational data flows through a system
- SQL — querying and joining operational data to surface insights (DataCamp Associate Data Analyst in SQL track, in progress)
- Rocxi Beach cost & profitability web app — built an internal tool (PostgreSQL/SQL, Next.js) to digitise manual cost and invoice processes, solving a real operational problem end to end
- Problem-solving in real-time under operational pressure (early hospitality experience)

## What to Downplay

- Luxury hospitality angle (unless the role is in a service business)
- Deep CRM segmentation detail (mention briefly)
- Content/design work (Canva, CapCut)

---

## Language to Use

- "Identified an operational inefficiency and designed a solution"
- "Mapped the current-state process and proposed improvements"
- "Built a dashboard to give management visibility over [specific operational area]"
- "Automated a recurring manual task to reduce friction and improve reliability"
- "Translated operational data into structured reporting"
- "Worked across multiple data sources to surface business insights"
- "Supported operational decision-making through..."

## Language to Avoid

- "Hospitality experience" as the main value claim
- "I am a hard worker"
- Listing tools without connecting them to operational outcomes

---

## ATS Keywords to Include

operations analysis, business operations, process mapping, process improvement, operational efficiency, workflow design, performance monitoring, operational KPIs, reporting automation, cross-functional, continuous improvement, data analysis, SQL, Excel, dashboard, automation
