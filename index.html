About
CMR Carla Manzano Ramells

Operations meet data.
Complexity becomes clarity.

Hospitality taught me that the real story is never on the surface. After years working within restaurant operations — managing reservations, tracking costs, watching service fall apart on busy Fridays — I became obsessed with understanding why things happen, not just that they do. That curiosity pulled me toward data: building dashboards to surface patterns management couldn't see, automating the processes eating up hours each week, and eventually designing the analytical systems that connect operational reality to business decisions.

Every project here started with a real problem in a real environment — not a dataset downloaded for a course. The work sits at the intersection of operations, analytics, and technology, built by someone who has spent time on both sides of the kitchen pass.

Portfolio — Section 5

Automation & Process Optimisation

Reducing repetitive manual work and improving operational efficiency through practical automation — emphasising systems thinking, workflow design, and operational friction reduction.

Problem

Manual Weekly Reports

Management spent 3+ hours each Monday consolidating sales, reservation, and feedback data from separate sources into a single report.

Solution

Automated Report Collection

Google Apps Script pulls data from multiple Sheets on a schedule, consolidates it, and emails a formatted summary report to management automatically every Monday morning.

Problem

Scattered Supplier Invoices

Supplier invoices arrived via email, WhatsApp, and paper — making cost tracking slow, error-prone, and impossible to analyse in aggregate.

Solution

Gmail-to-Drive Automation

Apps Script monitors a dedicated Gmail inbox, extracts invoice attachments, renames them with supplier and date metadata, and deposits them into structured Drive folders automatically.

Problem

CSV Processing Overhead

POS system exports required manual cleaning and formatting before they could be used in dashboards — a repetitive 45-minute task performed weekly.

Solution

CSV Processing Pipeline

Automated pipeline that detects new CSV exports, standardises column names, removes noise rows, formats dates, and loads clean data into the reporting sheet — reducing the task to zero manual effort.

The Approach

What Makes This Portfolio Different

Most junior portfolios focus on isolated academic projects or generic technical exercises. These projects originate from real operational environments with real constraints, real pressure, and real decision-making needs.

01
Business Thinking

Operations drive numbers, and numbers should guide operations. Every analysis is grounded in what the business actually needs to decide — not what is technically interesting.

02
Operational Understanding

Time on the floor informs every spreadsheet. Understanding why a metric behaves a certain way requires knowing what happens during a Friday dinner service.

03
Data as a Tool

Clean data, honest models, and actionable insights. The goal is clarity, not complexity — the best analysis is the one a busy manager can act on in three minutes.

04
AI as Augmentation

AI accelerates analysis and removes friction — it does not replace judgment. Knowing how to collaborate with AI effectively while maintaining business context is the modern professional skill.
