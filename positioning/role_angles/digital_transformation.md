# Role Angle: Digital Transformation

Use this file when applying to roles such as: Junior Digital Transformation Consultant, Junior Digitalization and Automation Technician, AI Adoption Assistant, AI Automation Junior Profile, SME Digitalization Specialist, Optima Grupo (Strategy and Transformation), OCA Global (Digitalization), Henkel (AI Adoption), Leadtech (AI Automation), BETWEEN Technology (Business and AI Automation).

---

## Core Pitch (3 sentences)

I developed a complete digital transformation roadmap for a real SME — not a case study — using Rocxi Beach as the business context, identifying digital maturity gaps, process inefficiencies, CRM opportunities, and automation use cases. I have built practical automation projects, worked with AI tools in business contexts, and understand the human and operational constraints that make transformation succeed or fail in small and medium-sized businesses. I am looking to apply this grounded digital transformation experience in a role where I can help organisations improve processes, adopt digital tools, and use data more effectively.

---

## What to Foreground

- Digital Transformation Roadmap (EAE) — complete SME plan, process mapping, maturity analysis, implementation thinking
- Gmail-to-Drive Automation (Google Apps Script) — designed and built a real automation from a real operational problem
- Rocxi Beach cost & profitability web app — built a working internal tool (Next.js, Supabase/PostgreSQL, SQL) with Claude Code to digitise manual cost and invoice processes
- AI-assisted analysis — practical, business-validated use of ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini for data, feedback, workflows
- Restaurant Operations Dashboard — moving from manual data to structured decision tools
- CRM improvement strategy — connecting digital tools to operational outcomes
- Understanding of SME constraints: limited time, messy data, non-technical teams, need for simple solutions

## What to Downplay

- Pure hospitality operations (frame as the operational context that makes transformation real, not as the main experience)
- Luxury hospitality (unless the role is in a luxury company)

---

## Language to Use

- "Developed a digital transformation roadmap for a real SME context"
- "Identified digital maturity gaps and designed practical improvement initiatives"
- "Built an automation to solve a recurring operational inefficiency"
- "Used AI tools to support business analysis and process improvement"
- "Mapped current-state processes and defined digital improvement opportunities"
- "Designed solutions that a non-technical team can actually use and maintain"
- "Translated operational pain points into digital improvement opportunities"
- "Built a working internal web app (Next.js, PostgreSQL) with AI assistance to digitise a manual process"
- "Understand the constraints of transformation in small businesses: time, data, team capacity"

## Language to Avoid

- "I am interested in AI" without grounding it in specific projects
- Technology-first framing that loses the business problem
- Claiming technical depth (coding, ML) beyond what the real experience supports
